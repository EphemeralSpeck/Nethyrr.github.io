What is Nethyrr

Nethyrr is a multiversee, that I've thought up myself. It consists of 7 components that make the foundation of it. First, there is The Primordial Plane, where all dimensions converge via the roots of the Dimension Tree Yggdrasil*. It then branches into 6 separate dimensions, each with their own unique characteristics. Right now most names are temporary these are marked with an Asterisk also anything with an asterisk on both sides isn't final. The roots of the Dimension Tree are the paths that lead to each dimension. The 6 other dimensions are the foundations of each universe in the multiverse. This happens through the roots of Yggdrasil* that end up touching, which in turn creates a whole universe that has characteristics of whatever universe/dimension that root was growing from.

The foundational dimensions are the first 6 dimensions, simply called the Core 6.
These are as Follows

  • *Elysium*
  • Rekzor
  • Arcanis*
  • Umbryss*
  • Elysara*
  • Anthenia

    • The basis of the Core 6 that I have cemented is that Rekzor is a dimension that is focused on technology. Arcanis* is a dimension focused on magic. Umbryss* is a dimension focused on dark magic. Elysara* is a dimension that is essentially a garden of Eden with no tree of knowledge. Anthenia* is a dimension focused on *concepts*.
